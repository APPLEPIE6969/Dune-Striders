<h1 align="center">Dune Striders 🏜️</h1>



<p align="center">
  <b>A lightweight vanilla+ mod that gives Armadillo Scutes a highly requested secondary use!</b>
</p>

## The Concept

Armadillo scutes currently only have one use: crafting Wolf Armor. This mod adds a brand new utility armor piece called **Dune Striders**, crafted directly from Armadillo Scutes.

When equipped, these boots grant the player a slight speed boost (Speed I) **only** when walking on:
- Sand
- Red Sand
- Suspicious Sand

## Why Dune Striders?

It perfectly fits the vanilla aesthetic and the savanna/badlands vibe of the Armadillo. The lightweight, tough composition of the scutes allows players to traverse desert environments rapidly, making exploring and excavating much more engaging!

## Features

- **New Item**: Dune Striders (Armadillo Scute Boots)
- **Crafting Recipe**: Crafted just like standard boots but using Armadillo Scutes (requires 4 scutes).
- **Dynamic Effect**: The Speed I buff is applied dynamically and feels vanilla-native, instantly boosting your speed as you stride across the dunes.
- **Vanilla Enchantability**: Fully compatible with normal boots enchantments at the Enchanting Table and Anvil (Mending, Unbreaking, Feather Falling, Depth Strider, etc.).
- **New 'Dune Speed' Enchantment**: A brand new custom enchantment specifically for the Dune Striders! It can be found on books or in the Enchanting Table, with a max tier of Level 3. Each level upgrades the dynamic sand speed buff by an extra degree (Dune Speed III grants Speed IV!).

## Compatibility

This mod is fully server-side compatible but required on the client for the items to show properly. It requires the Fabric API.
